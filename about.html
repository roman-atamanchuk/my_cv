Roman Atamanchuk

Hello! My name is Roman Atamanchuk, originally from Ukraine. Currently, I am based in Waterford, Ireland, where I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science at SETU University. My path has been both dynamic and enriching, blending my passion for technology, active living, and continuous learning.

Background & Studies

I was born in 1979 and have long been fascinated by the power of technology to shape our lives. This interest led me to pursue a formal education in Computer Science at SETU University, where I am developing skills in programming, problem-solving, and emerging technologies.

My Values & Passions

Lifelong learning and staying active are essential to me, both mentally and physically. I enjoy challenging myself in various areas, whether in coding projects or physical activities like jogging and swimming. Balancing these activities keeps me energized and motivated to continually grow.

Future Goals

My goal is to continue developing as a tech professional and explore fields like software engineering and data science. I aim to contribute to meaningful projects that solve real-world problems and create a positive impact in the technology sector.
